About the game
Roguelite Deckbulder With A Soul

Unlock new characters - Upgrade your island - Explore all the secrets of Forsaken Lands

In a world where technological progress prevails, аnd the power of untamed energy is harnessed by mages… Ordinary people have nothing left but to live their ordinary, carefree lives. Under the protection of the great gods, who watch from their heavenly realm.

Everything in this world existed in peace and harmony. Until someone decided to hog the cover... And the world turned into… the "Forsaken Lands"

features
Co-op up to 3 players

Sometimes you just want to snuggle up in a warm blanket on a chilly evening, sit at your monitor, and sip mulled wine while playing something captivating and relaxing with your friends... How about 'Forsaken Lands'? You can invite both your friends and find new ones in the server list!

features
Experience a roguelite-style narrative playthrough

In Forsaken Lands, you'll encounter a multitude of unique battles, dialogues, and adventures on your way to the goal.

features
Customize your personal island

Every traveler needs a place to relax, shine up their battle cards, polish their beloved relics, and give their tired backside a break. In your case, it will be a bar located on a floating piece of land.

features
Build YOUR team of heroes

What is a hero? It's not just a set of stats and unique cards; but a story of their own, unique abilities, and an altered playstyle as well. Try out each of the 10+ heroes and find the ones that will surely capture your heart :heart:

features
Collect loot!

Discover relics, unlock new cards, level up your characters, and uncover secret ways to gain maximum benefits from every event. Don't forget to spend your gold with the traders!
